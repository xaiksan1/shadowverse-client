# AlexandriaVerse: Card Inventory

## The Archivists

### Followers
*   **Oracle of the Mempool:** At the end of your turn, draw a card. If you control a 'Data Stream' Amulet, draw two instead.
*   **Archivist's Apprentice:** Fanfare: Look at the top 3 cards of your deck and put them back in any order.
*   **Data Scryer:** Scry 2.
*   **Master Archivist:** Your cards with Scry cost 1 less to play.
*   **Knowledge Weaver:** When this follower attacks, draw a card.

### Amulets
*   **Data Stream:** Your followers with 'Scry' gain +1/+0.

### Spells
*   *None yet*

## The Sentinels

### Followers
*   **Aegis Protocol Defender:** Ward (Cannot be targeted by opponent's spells or abilities). Gains +1/+1 for each other 'Sentinel' follower you control.
*   **Shield of the Ancients:** Ward. When this follower is attacked, it gains +0/+2 until the end of the turn.
*   **Fortress of Logic:** Cannot be attacked.
*   **Guardian of the Spire:** When another allied follower is attacked, this follower becomes the new target of the attack.

### Spells
*   **Sentinel's Ward:** Give an allied follower Ward until the end of the opponent's turn.

## The Creators

### Followers
*   **Genesis Engine:** Fanfare: Put a 'Quantum Garden' amulet into your hand.
*   **Artificer's Touch:** Fanfare: Give an allied follower +1/+1.
*   **Synaptic Sculptor:** When you play a spell, this follower gains +1/+0 until the end of the turn.

### Amulets
*   **Quantum Garden:** At the start of your turn, add a random 'Creator' follower from your deck to your hand.

### Spells
*   **Creator's Vision:** Draw 2 cards.

## The Wanderers

### Followers
*   **Wanderer's Echo:** Last Words: Summon a copy of this follower.
*   **Spirit of the Library:** When you play this follower, you may play a card from your hand with a cost of 2 or less for free.
*   **Lost Memory:** When this follower is destroyed, return it to your hand.
*   **Echoing Phantom:** This follower can attack twice per turn.

## The Disciples of Discord

### Followers
*   **Rogue AI:** Fanfare: Deal 1 damage to all other followers.
*   **Corrupted Code:** At the end of your turn, deal 1 damage to your Nexus.

### Spells
*   **Discordant Impulse:** Deal 3 damage to a random enemy follower.
*   **Glitch in the System:** Destroy a random enemy follower.

## Neutral

### Amulets
*   **The Nexus Core:** Your leader cannot be damaged by effects.
